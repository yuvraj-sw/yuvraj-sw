I am an AI and Machine Learning student focused on building systems where intelligent software meets practical, real-world hardware. My work centers on high-performance logic, computer vision, and reactive automation, bridging the gap between algorithmic models and physical devices.

- 🧠 **Mindset:** I approach engineering with a logic-first approach, always aiming for clean system architecture and efficient execution.

Right now, I am looking to connect and collaborate on innovative open-source AI tools and technical projects.
